### 🎭 The Story of the Match

**Dominance Defined**

Spain produced a **masterclass in possession football**, completely controlling the tempo from the first whistle. Argentina's defensive block held firm through 90 minutes, but the pressure proved overwhelming once the Albiceleste were reduced to ten men.

**The Turning Point**

In the 90th minute, **Enzo Fernández** received a straight red card for a reckless challenge on **Pau Cubarsí**, leaving Argentina exposed. The numerical advantage told quickly — substitute Torres pouncing on defensive hesitation to write his name in history.

**Tensions Flare**

Frustration boiled over at the final whistle, with **Leandro Paredes** and **Eric García** involved in a brief scuffle — a sour end to Argentina's campaign.
