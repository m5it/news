### <0xF0><0x9F>🐴 The Word Breakdown (Why it's confusing)
 
Spanish has different words that can refer to similar animals, depending on whether they are wild, domestic, or hybrid:
 
| Term | Refers To... | Usage/Context |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Burro** | Donkey (general term for the domesticated animal). | This is the most common word used in casual speech, but it can sometimes refer to a donkey that has been *tamed* or simply viewed as an object. |
| **Asno** | Wild Ass / Donkey (more formal/natural). | Used when discussing the species in its natural state or in serious writing. This is your safest bet. |
| **Mula** | Mule (the offspring of a male donkey and a female horse). | A distinct animal, not related to "wild." |
